About Clientura

Clientura is a non-profit initiative that offers 100% free beautician courses for women. We aim to provide practical, hands-on training to help women gain confidence, build skills, and become financially independent.

Our mission is to create a supportive learning environment where women can discover their potential and transform their lives through the art of beauty.
